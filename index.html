Marcus Bannerman

I am a lecturer in the School of Engineering at the University of Aberdeen who applies a wide range of computational techniques to solve a variety of engineering problems. I teach chemical engineering; however, I am also a prolific coder/hacker and thoroughly enjoy solving physics and chemistry problems. I also repair test equipment in my spare time.

Major codes (Github)

  • DynamO: website, Github
    An event-driven molecular dynamics code and visualisation suite.
  • Stator: Github
    A library for collision calculations and compile-time computer algebra library.
  • SimCem.com: website
    A database of thermodynamic data and corresponding computational thermodynamics code (Gibb's free energy minimisation).
